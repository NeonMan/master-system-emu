Opcodes that **do not** modify the Flag register.
-------------------------------------------------

8bit LD **Except**:
  LD A, I
  LD A, R
  
16bit LD. Neither do PUSH/POP.
EX and EXX.
NOP, HALT, DI, EI, IM.
16bit INC and DEC
SET and RES
Jump instructions, including DJNZ.
Call instructions, includeing RET/RETI/RST
IN A, (n). All other IN instructions **do** modify F.
OUT (n), A; OUT (C), r. All other OUT instructions **do** modify F
